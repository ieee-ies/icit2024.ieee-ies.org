Call for Tutorials

The duration of tutorials should be up to 2 hours. Prospective tutorial speakers are asked to submit a proposal up to two pages long, including:

  • Title of tutorial
  • The topics/content proposed for presentation, described through a narrative
  • Outline schedule of delivery (headings) for the 2 hours duration
  • Short biographies of all speakers

The tutorials will be selected based on merit, as well as relevance to technical areas which are of interest to the Industrial Electronics Society.

Tutorial proposal submission

The proposals should be sent as a .PDF file attachment to an e-mail directly to tutorial.chairs.icit2023@ieee-ies.org 

Important details to consider:

  • Tutorials will be held concurrently with other sessions, with a maximum of 6 tutorials offered.
  • Presenters of tutorials must register (one day or full registartion), and be present at the conference.
  • Attendees are required to register for the tutorials. A minimum of 10 registered attendees is necessary for tutorial to be included in the conference program.
